What is Tonkeeper Battery?

Tonkeeper Battery is an innovative feature designed to simplify and streamline digital transactions. It offers a seamless and efficient way to manage various types of digital assets and transactions, enhancing user experience with gasless payments and swaps.

Key Features

Gasless Payments and Swaps

  • USDT Transactions: Instantly send and receive USDT without worrying about transaction fees.
  • Notcoin and Other Tokens: Enjoy fee-free transactions with Notcoin and a variety of other digital tokens.

NFT Transfers

Seamless NFT Management: Easily transfer NFTs between wallets with no transaction costs, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Funding Options

  • In-App Purchases: Quickly fund your account using in-app purchase options.
  • Crypto Funding: Add funds directly from other crypto wallets, making it flexible and convenient.

Use Cases

Gasless Payments and Swaps

  • Convenience: Make transactions without the need to worry about gas fees.
  • Cost-Efficiency: Save money on every transaction by eliminating fees.
  • Speed: Enjoy faster transaction times with no gas fee processing delays.

NFT Transfers

  • Art and Collectibles: Easily transfer digital art and collectibles.
  • Gaming: Seamlessly move game assets between wallets and users.
  • Marketplaces: List and delist NFTs without additional costs.

Funding

  • Quick Access: Instantly fund your wallet through in-app purchases.
  • Flexibility: Top-up using various cryptocurrencies from different wallets.
